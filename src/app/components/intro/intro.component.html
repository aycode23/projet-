Introduction

Welcome to Securinets IsetCh, your gateway to the world of cybersecurity at Iset Charguia. Established in 2021, our club is a hub for cybersecurity enthusiasts,workshops, collaborative projects, and captivating Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges. Founded in 2003, Securinets stands as the largest cybersecurity association and community in Tunisia, boasting a professional technical team providing extensive annual training. Our strength lies in our diverse structure, which comprises three integral departments: Technical, Media, and Human Resources (RH). Each department plays a vital role in our community, contributing to a well-rounded and dynamic experience.
